Quality Control

Quality issues discovered after shipment are expensive — or impossible — to fix. Our quality control service catches problems at the source, before they become your problem.

Our inspectors are based on-site in China, meaning faster response times and real local expertise.

Inspection Types

  • Pre-production inspection (raw materials & components)
  • During production (DUPRO) — catches issues mid-run
  • Pre-shipment inspection (finished goods)
  • Container loading supervision

What You Receive

A detailed inspection report with photos, measurements, defect classification (critical / major / minor), and a clear pass/fail recommendation — all within 24 hours of inspection.

IP Protection

Intellectual property theft is a real risk in China manufacturing — but it's preventable with the right legal and contractual structures in place.

China operates on a first-to-file trademark system. If you haven't registered your brand in China, someone else can — and often does.

How We Protect You

  • NDA drafting in English and Mandarin (legally enforceable in China)
  • Guidance on China trademark registration
  • Split manufacturing strategy
  • Mould ownership agreements
  • Factory confidentiality agreements
  • IP audit before production begins

Protect your product before it's copied.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to work with you?

We offer a free initial consultation. Sourcing projects involve a flat project fee; quality inspections are priced per inspection day. Email contact@chinamerchants.info for a custom quote.

What's the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQs are set by factories. They vary widely — some accept 100 units, others require 1,000+. We help you negotiate MOQs down wherever possible.

How long does sourcing take?

Standard products: 2–4 weeks to identify and vet suppliers. Custom products with prototyping: 6–12 weeks before production begins.

Do you handle first-time importers?

Absolutely. Many clients are importing from China for the first time. We guide you step-by-step and explain the process clearly in plain English.
